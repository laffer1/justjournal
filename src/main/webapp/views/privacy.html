Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the JustJournal.com website. On some pages, you can create accounts, post publicly accesible journal entries and comments, and post other personal information. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:

First Name
Partial Address including State, Zip Code and Country
Email addresses
Phone number
Instant Messaging usernames
usernames nad passwords used on this site
personal diary & journal entries for public viewing

The Way We Use Information:

We use the information you provide about yourself to maintain your account. It may be used to contact you through e-mail about site announcements, password resets, and abuses.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.

You can register with our website if you would like to post publicly accesible journal entries or comments. In addition, journal entries marked private or friends only security will be protected to the best of our ability. It is possible through a failure or glitch in our software that this information could be viewable to other parties. We do not encrypt journal entries stored on our system, and if unauthorized access into our system could put your information at risk. Security measures are in place to prevent such occurances when possible. (including software patches, firewalls, antivirus, and file integrity software)

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, or that Y number of men and Z number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. Currently, we do not use advertising on our site for third parties. We may change this policy in the future.

Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. (we do not sell your e-mail addresses! Spam is a serious problem, and software is installed on our mail servers to prevent it.)

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. In the future we plan on adding SSL technology to this site and use of encryption technologies to protect sensitive information.

Our Commitment To Children's Privacy:

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by visiting the preferences page. You will need to login with your username and password. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information.

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error.

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.
